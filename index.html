Neurology

Our platform helps neurologists optimize treatment for patients with epilepsy, migraine, and neurodegenerative disorders by predicting individual responses to medications and minimizing adverse effects.

Cardiologists use PharmaSage to optimize therapeutic choices for patients with hypertension, arrhythmias, and heart failure. The platform integrates medication history, comorbidities, and genetic factors to predict optimal drug combinations.

Clinical studies show a 28% reduction in adverse cardiovascular events when treatments are guided by our AI recommendations.
